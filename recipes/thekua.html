Thekua Recipe

Description

Thekua is a traditional deep-fried sweet snack from Bihar made from whole wheat flour (aata).
Thekua is offered as prasad during the festival of Chhath Puja celebrated in Bihar.

Ingredients

  • Whole wheat flour (aata)
  • Ghee
  • Jaggery
  • Water
  • Raisins
  • Cashew Nuts

Steps

  1. Heat jaggery with water to form a syrup.
  2. Add melted ghee, cashew nuts and raisins to the whole wheat flour.
  3. Mix them thoroughly for five minutes.
  4. Add the jaggery syrup to the mixture and mix it into a dough with no lumps.
  5. Add ghee to a pan and let it heat
  6. Use the dough to prepare small round balls and press them to make them flat
  7. Finally, drop them into the ghee pan and fry them till they become reddish brown in color.
  8. Enjoy the delicious thekuas.
